Hi there! My name is Junfeng Liao, working as a visiting student at Mohamed bin Zayed University of Artificial Intelligence (MBZUAI). This August, I will start my research journey to pursue my PhD degree at MBZUAI, supervised by Prof. Xiuying Chen. Before that, I was a Research Assistant at the University of Technology Sydney, working with Dr. Qizhou Wang, Prof. Zhen Fang, and Prof. Ling Chen. I also hold a Master's degree in Applied Statistics from the Shanghai University of International Business and Economics, advised by Prof. Riquan Zhang.

My research goal is to achieve auto-everything with Agentic AI. And I am particularly interested in Agent Memory and Agentic Reinforcement Learning. My earlier research focused on LLM unlearning and alignment to make LLMs adaptable to dynamic world criteria. I am always eager to explore new ideas and collaborate with researchers with diverse backgrounds to undertake something awesome for the community. If you wanna chat with me, please don't hesitate to reach out via email.
